Hello and welcome to the website of Pixellod, a Dutch creative graphic design company located in Sliedrecht, Holland. Pixellod is 'a one man show' founded and run by me, Gert Jan Lodder. I'm working with clients all around the world and my specialty is icon and UI design.
 

What I do

  
   

I'm dedicated to creation. Creation is a process, an exploration through goals, dreams, culture, taste and ambition. That's why I excel in translating ideas into great designs. In order to achieve the best possible result I work in a transparent way. For me it's crucial to find the right and most productive way to cooperate with the client. Identifying and profiling enables me to make designs that fit the client seamlessly. I work in close cooperation with my clients with a focus on the clients of my client. I design clean, unique and pixel-perfect icons, UI's, UX's, logos, corporate identities and webs. My motto is: "Price is what you pay. Value is what you get." So if you're looking for a good investment and quality don't search any longer and mail me for a surprising quote!
